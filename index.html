UX/UI Case Studies

Findflix

Findflix focuses on creating a streamlined experience for a user that uses streaming apps to watch movies, we all struggle with scrolling through available programs. Findflix filters and compiles all options in one spot.

My role

User Research, User Interface Design

Goal

The goal of this app was to streamline the process of finding movies to watch on streaming platforms, save money and time in the process.

Tools

Adobe XD, Miro, InVision, Adobe Illustrator, Slack

Overview

This project was a mobile app creation that focused on solving a users streaming experience. We all experience endless scrolling when looking for a movie to watch on streaming platforms, this solves it!

My Role

In this project I focused on developing the user research in order to guide the design of the app. I wanted to add value with a social aspect and bookmarking movies in order to add to your playlist, I created the logo, as well as the general look and feel of the design. I designed the app in dark mode to bring in the feeling of theater and movie streaming to the design aesthetic.

User Research

I created a research plan objectives to be able to focus our questions in order to receive the information we are looking for. Our objectives were to find out to what a user is experiencing when adopting a new pet, any concerns and pain points. To understand what they are looking for.

Research Analysis

We analyzed our user research data by creating an affinity diagram, by doing this we grouped the information and started to connect the similarities and defining the pain points, overall feedback and user needs. Our key takeaways from our grouping was that a user is interested in filtering, having an ability to watch and schedule movies with friends, sharing, and streaming users were frustrated with trying to remember and stay up to date with streaming platforms.

User Flow

While creating the user flow I focused on the onboarding as well as playlist and calendar feature flows. This is a new app that a user would need a tutorial in order to be properly onboarded in the app. The user also needs to create an account and sign in to have the ability to search all their platforms. Ideation

My team and I created a User Persona that outlines a perspective and goal for a more specific user that would be experiencing the website. Our user focused on having multiple streaming platforms that enjoys spending time with friends and family and having movie nights. She would like to be able to filter her movie options and know what streaming platform that she currently is subscribed to has it for free. She is very frustrated with finding the movies through scrolling or searching multiple times.
